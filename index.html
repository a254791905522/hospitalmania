Hospital Mania Hidden Object

Find objects. Save lives—fast.

Free to play · iPhone & iPad · Requires iOS 15.0 or later · English · Game Center

Search, discover, and heal in a fast-paced hospital adventure. Find hidden objects in richly drawn scenes, beat the clock, and work through fun medical challenges in a bright, busy hospital full of details and surprises.

What you will do

  • Explore the hospital — From patient rooms and operating theaters to labs and emergency areas, every stage is built around observation and care.
  • Hunt for hidden items — Cleverly placed objects reward sharp eyes across colorful, hand-crafted layouts.
  • Race against time — Find everything before the timer runs out to keep the pressure and focus high.
  • Always something new — New items and level layouts help keep every round fresh and replayable.
  • Easy, relaxed play — Simple touch controls, smooth feedback, and a clean interface built for short sessions at home or on the go.

The game is rated 4+ and contains advertising. Leaderboards and Game Center features add friendly competition for players who enable them on their device.
